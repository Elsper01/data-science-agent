summary="This dataset represents the official 'Denkmalliste' (List of Cultural Monuments) for the state of Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. It comprises 11,172 registered monuments, ranging from individual buildings ('Bauliche Anlagen') to complex ensembles ('Sachgesamtheiten') and historical gardens ('Gründenkmale'). The data is managed by the State Office for Monument Preservation (Landesamt für Denkmalpflege). A significant characteristic of this dataset is its transitional nature: following a 2015 legislative change, many entries (approx. 49% in the 'Beschreibung' column) are marked as 'Alteintragung', indicating they are legacy records awaiting updated descriptions and protection scopes. Geographically, the dataset shows a high concentration of monuments in Nordfriesland and the city of Flensburg. The 'Begründung' column provides insight into the valuation criteria, with 'Historical' and 'Urban Planning' significance being the most frequent justifications.
