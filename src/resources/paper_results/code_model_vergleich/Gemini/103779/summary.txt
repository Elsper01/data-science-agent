It comprises 371 registered cultural monuments, ranging from individual buildings (Bauliche Anlagen) to green spaces (Gründenkmale) and ensembles (Sachgesamtheiten). A significant portion of the data (approx. 50%) contains placeholder descriptions ('Alteintragung'), indicating that while these objects are legally protected under the 2015 Monument Protection Act, their detailed documentation is currently undergoing administrative updates. Geographically, the dataset is concentrated in Bad Segeberg (17.5% of entries), with the remaining monuments distributed across 64 other municipalities. The data provides insights into the regional heritage, dominated by residential buildings and historical estates (Gutshöfe). Notably, only about 33% of the entries include a photo URL, suggesting a gap in digital visual documentation.
